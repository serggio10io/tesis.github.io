Present Perfect vs. Past Simple

1. She ________ (live) in London for five years before moving to Paris.

had lived
has lived
lived
was living

2. I ________ (never/visit) Japan, but I would like to go someday.

never visited
have never visited
had never visited
am never visiting

3. ________ you ________ (finish) your homework yet?

Did, finish
Were, finishing
Have, finished
Had, finished
